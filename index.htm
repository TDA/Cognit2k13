Contraversia

Rules:
  • There must be 4 participants in a team.
  • Teams will be assigned to defend or oppose the statement based on the flip of a coin.
  • Teams will get to choose their topics from a lot ,based on the coin toss. Winning team will be asked to choose the topic and also to decide whether they are going to talk for or against the topic.
  • 2minutes for preparation .Beginning with the defending team, each team will have 4 minutes to present their position. Totally 10 minutes will be given for both teams to complete their debate.
  • Each and every participant must be loud and clear in their points.
  • Repetition of the same points would be disqualified.
  • When there is a knock out between the two teams, you may be allowed to argue but there should be no quarrelling between teams if so, that particular team would be disqualified.
  • Participants can talk when chance is given,if not they would be disqualified.
  • All participants in a team must be given chance to talk, if not they would be disqualified.
  • A warning bell will be given at the end, it means that the participants must conclude their position.
